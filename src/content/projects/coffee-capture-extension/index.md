A companion browser extension to [Distilled](/projects/distilled). When I find a cafe or coffee worth adding to the database, I can capture it directly from the browser without switching to a separate interface.

Built to remove friction from the curation workflow — the extension handles the data entry, Supabase stores it, and Distilled surfaces it.
