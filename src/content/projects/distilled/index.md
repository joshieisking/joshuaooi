A specialty coffee discovery app I'm building primarily for my own use — a curated database of cafes and single-origin beans, with brew tracking over time. The curation is manual and deliberate: I'm building the content layer as I go.

Companion to this is a browser extension ([coffee-capture-extension](/projects/coffee-capture-extension)) that makes adding new entries faster while browsing.

Built with Astro on the frontend and Supabase for the backend.
